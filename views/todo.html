Amenities

Whether you're here to unwind or explore, our amenities are designed to make your stay effortless and enjoyable.

Complimentary Breakfast

Each morning, enjoy a complimentary breakfast served from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM in our cozy dining area. Savor fresh local pastries, seasonal fruit, and a selection of beverages to start your day the right way!

Wi-Fi

Stay connected during your stay with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel.

On-Site Parking

Free on-site parking is available for all guests during your stay.

Guest Lounge

Relax and unwind in our comfortable guest lounge, where you can enjoy reading, games, or simply relax with a warm beverage.

Bicycles

We offer complimentary bicycle rentals to explore the beautiful surroundings of the San Juan Islands.
